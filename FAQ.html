General Questions

Can I cancel or updated my order before it is ready for shipping?

Yes. To cancel or update an order, please call our product specialist team at 604-123-4567.

How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, you will receive an email containing your tracking number. If for any reason you have not received your shipping confirmation email, please let us know by emailing askme@wbbdrink.ca. We will be happy to find out what exactly is going on with your order.

Shipping

How long will my shipment take to arrive?

We ship by Fedex or UPS Ground which takes around 3 days to most places throughout the Canada. We aim to get every order out within 48-72 hours after receiving it.

Return & Exchange

Can I exchange the purchased product?

For an exchange of a purchased product, you must present the original sales receipt and the unopened and un-tampered product. It must be no more than 60 days since the date of purchase.

Is it possible to refund the purchased product?

For refund of a purchased product, you must present the original sales receipt and the unopened and un-tampered product. It must be no more than 60 days since the date of purchase. All refunds over $100 are subject to a 13% restocking fee.
